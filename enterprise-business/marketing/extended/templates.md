## Content Calendar Template

### Weekly Content Planning Example

```
Monday: Motivation/start-of-week energy (quotes, goal setting)
Tuesday: Educational content (how-to, tips)
Wednesday: User testimonials/case studies
Thursday: Behind-the-scenes/team introductions
Friday: Interactive posts (Q&A, polls)
Saturday: Entertaining/light content
Sunday: Reflection/next week preview
```

## Brand Guidelines Template

### Brand Voice Guide

```markdown
## Brand Voice Definition

### Brand Personality
Our brand is like: [describe a personified character]

### Tone Traits
| We are... | We are not... |
|-----------|-------------|
| Friendly but professional | Overly formal |
| Concise but not cold | Full of jargon |
| Confident but not arrogant | Condescending |
| Humorous but tasteful | Flippant |

### Language Guidelines
| Use | Avoid |
|------|------|
| You | Formal address (unless in formal contexts) |
| Simple and clear | Professional jargon |
| Active voice | Passive voice |

### Tone Example

Scenario: Customer inquiry

❌ Bad response:
"Per our policy, your request must go through the review process."

✅ Good response:
"Got it! We'll get back to you within 24 hours — feel free to ask us anything :)"
```
