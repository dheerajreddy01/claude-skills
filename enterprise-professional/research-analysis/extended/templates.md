## User Interview Guide

```markdown
## Opening (5 minutes)
- Introduce yourself
- Explain the purpose and confidentiality
- Confirm consent to record

## Warm-up Questions (5 minutes)
- Could you briefly introduce your work/background?

## Core Questions (30-40 minutes)
### Understanding Behavior
- How do you typically handle [task]?
- When was the last time [scenario] happened? Can you describe it?

### Uncovering Pain Points
- What was the biggest difficulty in the process?
- Was there anything that felt frustrating?

### Exploring Needs
- If you could change one thing, what would it be?
- What would the ideal solution look like?

## Wrap-up (5 minutes)
- Is there anything else you'd like to add?
- Thank the participant
```

## Interview Analysis Coding Process

```
1. Open Coding
   - Mark key concepts sentence by sentence
   - Record original language

2. Axial Coding
   - Group similar concepts together
   - Establish relationships between categories

3. Selective Coding
   - Identify core themes
   - Construct a theoretical framework
```
