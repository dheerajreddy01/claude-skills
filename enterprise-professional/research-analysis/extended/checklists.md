# Research Analysis Checklists

## Research Ethics Checklist

### Must Follow

- [ ] Obtain informed consent
- [ ] Protect participant privacy
- [ ] Report data honestly
- [ ] Cite data sources
- [ ] State research limitations

### Avoid

- Manipulating data to fit expected conclusions
- Selectively presenting favorable results
- Using personal data without consent
- Overstating the generalizability of findings

## Research Quality Assessment

### Qualitative Research Quality Standards

| Standard | Description | How to achieve it |
|------|------|----------|
| **Credibility** | Whether the findings are trustworthy | Triangulation, member checking |
| **Transferability** | Whether results can be applied to other contexts | Thick description, clear context |
| **Dependability** | Whether the process is traceable and reproducible | Detailed records, audit trail |
| **Confirmability** | Whether researcher bias is controlled for | Reflexive journal, peer review |

### Quantitative Research Quality Standards

| Standard | Description | Evaluation method |
|------|------|----------|
| **Internal validity** | Whether the causal relationship holds | Control variables, random assignment |
| **External validity** | Whether results can be generalized | Sample representativeness |
| **Reliability** | Whether measurement is consistent | Cronbach's alpha |
| **Construct validity** | Whether the measure captures what it's meant to | Factor analysis |

## AI-Assisted Research

### AI Applications in Research

| Stage | AI application | Tools |
|------|---------|------|
| **Literature review** | Paper summarization, topic clustering | Elicit, Semantic Scholar |
| **Interview analysis** | Transcription, coding suggestions | Otter.ai, Claude |
| **Questionnaire analysis** | Open-ended response classification | Claude, GPT |
| **Report writing** | Draft generation, summarization | Claude, GPT |

### AI Usage Principles

**Do's**
- Use AI to speed up preliminary analysis
- Let AI suggest codes, with human confirmation
- Use AI to generate drafts, with human revision
- Label AI-assisted portions

**Don'ts**
- Rely entirely on AI for final judgments
- Adopt AI analysis results without verification
- Use AI to generate fabricated data
- Conceal AI's role in the research

## Pre-Research Checklist

- [ ] Is the research question clear and answerable?
- [ ] Is the research method suitable for answering the research question?
- [ ] Is the sample size sufficient?
- [ ] Has the necessary ethical review been obtained?
- [ ] Are the data collection tools ready?
- [ ] Is the timeline reasonable?

## Post-Research Checklist

- [ ] Was the data collection process fully documented?
- [ ] Was the analysis method applied correctly?
- [ ] Were the research limitations honestly disclosed?
- [ ] Are the conclusions supported by the data?
- [ ] Are the recommendations specific and actionable?
- [ ] Is the report clear and readable?
