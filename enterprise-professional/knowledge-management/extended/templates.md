## Daily/Weekly/Monthly/Quarterly Workflow Checklists

### Daily (5-10 minutes)
- Clear inboxes (email, notes, tasks)
- Process fleeting notes
- Confirm today's priorities

### Weekly (30-60 minutes)
- [ ] Weekly review
- [ ] Organize this week's notes
- [ ] Plan next week's priorities
- [ ] Process accumulated "read later" items

### Monthly (1-2 hours)
- [ ] Monthly review
- [ ] Review project progress
- [ ] Organize and archive
- [ ] Update goals and priorities

### Quarterly (half a day)
- [ ] Quarterly deep review
- [ ] Assess progress on annual goals
- [ ] Adjust systems and workflows
- [ ] Clean up outdated content
- [ ] Plan focus areas for next quarter
```
