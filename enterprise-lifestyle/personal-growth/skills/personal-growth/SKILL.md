### Brand Elements

```markdown
## Personal Brand Canvas

### Core Message
- **One-line positioning**: I help [who] solve [what problem] through [what approach]
- **Keywords**: 3-5 words that represent you
- **Differentiation**: What makes you different from others?

### Content Strategy
- **Topic areas**: Focus on 2-3 topics
- **Content formats**: Articles/Videos/Podcast/Social media
- **Publishing cadence**: A sustainable rhythm

### Visual Identity
- **Avatar**: Professional and consistent
- **Colors**: 1-2 primary colors
- **Style**: Formal/Friendly/Professional/Playful

### Platform Selection
| Platform | Best for | Content type |
|------|------|----------|
| LinkedIn | B2B, professional | Long-form posts, insights |
| Twitter/X | Tech, ideas | Short posts, interaction |
| Instagram | Lifestyle | Photos, short videos |
| YouTube | Tutorials, sharing | Long-form videos |
| Blog | SEO, in-depth content | Long-form articles, tutorials |
```

### Steps to Build a Personal Brand

```markdown
## 90-Day Personal Brand Launch Plan

### Days 1-30: Positioning

- [ ] Analyze what content performs best
```

### Pomodoro Technique

```markdown
## Pomodoro Technique

1. **Choose a task** - Decide what to work on
2. **Set 25 minutes** - Focus on the work
3. **Take a 5-minute break** - Step away from the screen
4. **Every 4 pomodoros** - Take a 15-30 minute break

Advanced tips:
- Track your daily pomodoro count
- Estimate how many pomodoros a task will take
- Log interruptions and improve over time
```
